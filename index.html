Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how the Chrome Extension "Minar e Perder" collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

This extension does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information. The only data stored locally includes user preferences such as language, configuration settings, and Telegram Chat ID if provided by the user.

2. Permissions Justification

  • Storage: Used to save user preferences such as thresholds and Telegram ID.
  • Notifications: Required to show price alerts to the user.
  • Tabs: Allows the extension to know the active tab for context-aware features.
  • Scripting: Used to inject scripts that help monitor and notify price changes.
  • Host permissions (rollercoin.com): The extension runs only on the RollerCoin marketplace to fetch product and pricing information.
